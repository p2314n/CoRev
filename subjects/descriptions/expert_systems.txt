Unit 1: Introduction to Expert Systems 
Expert Systems Definition and Applications: Understanding the fundamentals of expert 
systems, their role in AI, and real-world applications in various industries. History and 
Evolution of Expert Systems. Components of an Expert System: knowledge base, inference 
engine, user interface, explanation facility etc. Expert System vs Conventional Systems: 
Architecture and Functionalities 

Unit 2: Knowledge Acquisition and Representation 
Knowledge Acquisition: Methods and tools for acquiring knowledge from experts, including 
interviewing techniques and the use of knowledge acquisition software. Knowledge 
Engineering: Process of designing and building expert systems, with a focus on the roles and 
responsibilities of a knowledge engineer. Structured Knowledge Representation: Exploring 
structured techniques for representing knowledge specifically in expert systems, focusing on 
production rules and decision tables. Ontologies in Expert Systems.

Unit 3: Inference Techniques and Rule-Based Systems
Inference Mechanisms: Forward chaining and backward chaining. Rule-Based Systems: 
Structure, Function, and application in expert systems, Broader knowledge representation.
Case-Based Reasoning, Expert System Architectures: Different architectural approaches for 
designing expert systems, including modular, distributed, and blackboard architectures.

Unit 4:
Expert System Development Tools: Expert System Shells: Overview of various expert 
system shells like CLIPS, Jess, and Drools.
Building Expert Systems: knowledge base creation, rule development, testing etc. Evaluation 
and Validation: Techniques for evaluating the performance and accuracy of expert systems, 
and methods for validating the correctness. Maintenance of Expert Systems.

Unit 5: 
Advanced Topics and Future Trends 
Integration with other Technologies like IoT and big data analytics. Scalability and 
Performance. Ethical and Social Implications including issues of trust, accountability, and job 
displacement. Future Trends in Expert Systems including advancements in adaptive and selflearning systems,
 and emerging applications in various fields. Case Studies and Industry 
Applications.
